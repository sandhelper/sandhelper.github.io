Sand Helper Locations

Sand Helper rents the Sand Scooter II on beaches from New Jersey to South Carolina. Use the included maps to pan and zoom to see if Sand Helper is at your summer beach spot. Click on the Sand Helper icon to see how to obtain rentals and for pricing. A Sand Scooter II will be delivered to and picked up from your beach home, with some beaches having two rental agencies to meet your mobility needs.
