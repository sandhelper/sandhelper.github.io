Surf's up -- we're off to the beach. Everyone! You no longer have to be left behind. With Sand Helper, everyone makes it to the surf's edge. The Sand Scooter II, a patent-pending, four-wheel drive, electric, beach wheelchair, can navigate the beach with ease. You won't believe the power.

Based out of Ocean City, MD, Sand Helper partners with rental companies up and down the East Coast to make the Sand Scooter II available on many beaches. Leave beach accessibility to Sand Helper!

Sand Helper's Story

In the summer of 2015, the inspiration for Sand Helper was born. When his aging grandmother could no longer join in on the family's annual beach vacation, Nick Ousborne was driven with purpose. For next year's summer vacation, Nick -- the founder and owner of Sand Helper and a proud University of Maryland graduate in mechanical engineering -- designed, tested and manufactured a wheelchair that would ferry his grandmother with ease. Other beach-goers expressed a desire for the one-of-a-kind, rugged machine they saw rolling across the sand. Thereafter, Nick established Sand Helper to assist others in attaining the joy of going to the beach.

Customer beach accessibililty continues to take center stage for Sand Helper.
