Sand Helper's Story

Surf's up -- we're off to the beach! Everyone! You no longer have to be left behind. With Sand Helper, everyone makes it to the surf's edge. The Sand Scooter, a patent-pending four-wheel- drive electric motorized beach wheelchair, can navigate the beach with ease. You won't believe its capability.

If you're at the beach, you should be able to get onto the beach. This concept is exactly why Sand Helper became a reality. The idea for the Sand Scooter was sparked by one Maryland family's love for the beach. Since the 1950s, the growing extended family has visited Ocean City, Maryland for a vacation every summer, but eventually the grandparents began to struggle walking through the soft sand. After a couple of years and several unpleasant experiences pushing the manually powered beach wheelchairs, one family member decided enough was enough - it was time for some power. After several revisions and countless improvements to the wheelchair over the years, it was realized that the device they had created, which was ready for commercial use, would surely be desired by other beach-goers seeking independence.

Today, Sand Helper has grown into a noteworthy company, serving plenty of beaches on the East Coast, with persistent plans for expansion. With rental locations in Maryland, Delaware, New Jersey, Virginia, the Carolinas, and Florida, beach accessibility continues to take center stage for Sand Helper.
