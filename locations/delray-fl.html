About this Location

If you or a family member struggles to walk through the sand, look no further! Sand Helper now offers rentals of our powered beach wheelchair at several beaches in Southeast Florida. Reach the beach in Florida without breaking a sweat! Our West Palm location delivers the chair right to your vacation residence, whether you're staying in West Palm, Boynton, Delray, Highland, Boca Raton, Deerfield, or Pompano Beach. Rent this state-of-the-art beach wheelchair and we will deliver it right to your door! Our wheelchair is battery-powered and controlled by a simple- to- operate joystick. You no longer need to be confined to your hotel. Drive this beach wheelchair onto the sand and join your friends and family for some quality time. You can reserve online (below) or give us a call. Don't forget we have numerous locations so feel free to inquire about your favorite beach, even if you don't see it here!
