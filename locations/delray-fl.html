About this Location

If you are heading to any of these beautiful beaches in Southeast Florida, and you or a family member have difficulty walking through the sand, look no further! Sand Helper now offers rentals of our powered beach wheelchair at several beaches in Southeast Florida. This is our newest delivery area! Rent a beach wheelchair and we will deliver it right to your door! Our wheelchair is battery-powered and controlled by a simple- to-operate joystick. You no longer need to be confined to the streets. Drive this beach wheelchair onto the sand and join your friends and family for some beach time. You can reserve online (below) or give us a call. Don’t forget we have many other locations so feel free to inquire about your favorite beach even if you don’t see it here!
