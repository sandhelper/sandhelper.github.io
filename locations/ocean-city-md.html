About this Location

Welcome to Ocean City MD, the original Sand Helper location where the Sand Helper motorized beach wheelchair was created. Cruise along the beach with the all-terrain Sand Scooter, which will surely give you the independence you deserve. Rent this battery-powered beach scooter and you'll be accessing the beach with the push of a simple-to-operate joystick. No prior experience required! This location also delivers to Fenwick DE, Bethany Beach, and Assateague Island at no additional cost. Beach access is not the only thing we offer at this location. Rent a portable scooter or regular wheelchair and you'll getting around town with minimal effort. The portable scooter breaks down into 4 lightweight pieces, which you can place in the back of any car and take it to the boardwalk or wherever else you want to spend your time. Don't forget that the Sand Helper wheelchair can also be driven on hard surfaces such as sidewalks and the boardwalk!
