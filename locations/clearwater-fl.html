About this Location

Come make some memories at any of the wonderful beaches in the Tampa, Florida area, but not without the help of your beach wheelchair! Our beach wheelchair is battery-operated and makes accessing the beach a flawless event for a person who has any level of difficulty traversing the soft sand. When you rent our beach wheelchair in Clearwater, St Petersburg, or any of the nearby beaches, it's a guarantee that you will enjoy the independence it offers you.
